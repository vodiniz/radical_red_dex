How to Receive Focus Sash/Specs/Scarf etc. from quests in Minimal Grinding Mode?
Instead show a Pokemon with 125+ base stat in the respective stat (Chuck asks for a pokemon with strong attack, show a mon with 125+ base attack like terrakion)

Some Boss Pokemon have access to illegal moves, but they aren’t anything too outrageous (some examples include Grassy Glide Tapu Bulu, High Horsepower Gliscor)
